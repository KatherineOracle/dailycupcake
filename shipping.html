Shipping policy

We promise to deliver your cupcakes the day they are ordered, and we aim to deliver within two hours.

Orders at the Daily Cupcake open at 5am and close at 4pm each day, while those who have activated cupcake alerts on specific items will get a secret link to pre-order from 12pm the day prior.

Our ovens are located in the heart of Sandton, and we can only delivery within a 15km thereof if we are to meet our delivery commitments to our valued customers. When you place your order our smart form will not allow you select a delivery address outside of this area.

What if we mess up?

As you know, on occasion, life doesn't go the way we expected it to and throws in factors beyond one's control.

If this happens and we can't delivery on time, we can either refund you or give you voucher to order again in future. When you do order again, we will include a little gift to affirm how sorry we are for having let you down.
