About us

Our philosophy – flavour first, always!

We've all had this experience of wanting cake, getting it, (it looked so pretty, right), but after the first few bites all you have in your mouth is that slick, heavy taste of regret. Why is this? Poor quality ingredients, that's why. At the Daily cupcake we promise to never waste your calorie allowance as we only use the highest quality, and freshest ingredients when we bake. Our cupcakes not only look good, they taste like heaven.

A Cake whim can strike at any moment!

... and that's when we are here to save the day!

Its also why we only create one type of cupcake per day – so we can throw all our love into making something special that is also fresh and ready to deliver.

We are Jozi-based

Our ovens are located in the heart of Sandton, and we delivery within a 15km radius thereof to ensure that we can delivery the same say as per our delivery policy.

Do we cater for vegans and those with gluten and other allergies?

... sometimes.

The qualities of vegan or gluten-free ingredients do sometimes lend themselves best to cupcake of the day, in which case the answer on that particular day would yes. We do aim to make at least one cupcake a week that is vegan or gluten-free.

If you are vegan or gluten intolerant, we suggest you browse our cupcake 'Hall of Famea>' on our home page and activate 'Cupcake alerts' for the cupcakes that suit your dietary requirements. THis way you'll be first in line to place your order the next time theseare born from our kitchen.

What do we do if we have cupcakes left over at the end of a day?

We donate these to local orphanages and child-headed households.
